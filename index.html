Introduction

Hi, my name is Temi and I am a sophomore at NYU studying Communications. I was born in Nigeria and now live in Bethesda, Maryland. This website is a space where I share more about my background, my interests, and the things that inspire me. It’s a personal project that reflects who I am and what I enjoy most.

What you'll learn here

On this website, you’ll learn about my background, the journey that brought me from Nigeria to Maryland, and my experiences as a Communications student at NYU. You’ll also get to know what inspires me, what I’m passionate about, and the activities that bring me joy.

My Interests

I enjoy staying active through pilates and going to the gym, and I also love cooking and trying out new recipes. Exploring cafes and discovering cozy spots in the city are some of my favorite things to do, and I always cherish spending time with friends.
